## MARKET CONTEXT: WHY EINDHOVEN?

### The Eindhoven Opportunity

**City profile:**

- Population: 240,000+ (Eindhoven proper)
- Metro area: 1.2M+ (within 40 km radius)
- Income level: High (tech hub, manufacturing, professional services)
- Wedding market: Estimated 2,500–3,500 weddings/year in region
- Corporate events: 1,500+ annually (Philips, NXP, ASML, startups, consulting firms)

**Why it's a prime market:**

1. **High discretionary income** - Couples and corporations can afford premium entertainment
2. **Tech hub culture** - Sophisticated audiences, willingness to invest in experience
3. **Regional centrality** - Natural hub for weddings/events across North Brabant
4. **Venue density** - 50+ premium venues within 40 km radius
5. **Limited local competition** - Few premium local vocalists focus on this market strategically

**Our strategy:** Become the **default premium vocalist choice** in Eindhoven region by dominating local search, partnerships, and reputation.
