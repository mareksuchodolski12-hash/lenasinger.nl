## 90-Day Strategic Framework (Weeks 1–12)

**Document Version:** 1.0

**Market:** Eindhoven + 40 km radius (North Brabant, Netherlands)  
**Objective:** Become the default premium vocalist choice in the region

---

## STRATEGIC PILLARS

### 1. LOCAL SEO DOMINATION

- **23 city pages** targeting wedding + corporate keywords within 40 km radius
- **Tier 1 (Weeks 1–2):** 6 priority cities (Veldhoven, Best, Nuenen, Geldrop, Waalre, Helmond)
- **Tier 2–4 (Weeks 3+):** Remaining 17 cities in phased rollout
- **Expected outcome:** 15+ top-10 rankings within 3 months

### 2. POSITIONING CLARITY

- **Wedding segment:** "Emotional storytelling + stress-free coordination for couples 25–38"
- **Corporate segment:** "Professional entertainment as brand strategy for tech/professional services"
- **Unified message:** "Premium, reliable, local expertise in North Brabant venues"

### 3. PRICING ARCHITECTURE

- **Wedding tiers:** Essential (€2,200), Premium (€3,800), Signature (€5,500)
- **Corporate tiers:** Acoustic (€2,000), Extended (€3,500), Branded (€5,000)
- **Pricing filters:** High price = high-intent leads only
- **Strategic goal:** Premium positioning attracts serious, profitable customers

### 4. FUNNEL OPTIMIZATION

- **Multi-step booking form** (event type → date → city → budget)
- **Lead scoring system** (urgent = < 60 days, high-budget = > €4,000)
- **Automated nurture sequences** (confirmation email, follow-up, value delivery)
- **Strategic goal:** Convert 30%+ of qualified leads to bookings

### 5. CONTENT CLUSTER STRATEGY

- **6-month content roadmap** (12+ blog posts supporting local keywords)
- **Keyword clusters:** Wedding ceremony, reception music, corporate events, North Brabant venues
- **Hub content:** Main services pages
- **Spoke content:** City pages + blog posts interlinking back to hubs
- **Strategic goal:** Organic traffic increase, keyword authority, backlinking ecosystem
